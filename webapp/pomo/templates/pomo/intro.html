Welcome to Arcadia!

Congratulations on taking your first step into the world of magic and focus! At Arcadia, you’ll navigate through a magical school using an enchanted map. The map shows different locations, such as the Library, Great Hall, and Gardens, each offering unique events or tasks for you to complete.

When you choose an event, you’ll embark on a challenge tied to the Pomodoro technique. This means you’ll focus on your studies in the real world while the magic of Arcadia helps you stay motivated. A timer will guide your session—25 minutes of focused work, followed by a short break. Once the timer is complete, you’ll return to the story, discovering what happens next and potentially unlocking new events or locations.

Remember, some events are only available for a limited time or may affect what happens next in your journey. Choose wisely and stay committed to mastering both your studies and the magic of Arcadia.
